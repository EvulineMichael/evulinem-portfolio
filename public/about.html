Here's a look at a project that holds a special place in my heart:

BP Analyzer

The BP Analyzer is a website I developed to assist individuals, particularly my parents, in monitoring and understanding their blood pressure readings. The website provides detailed guidelines on how to properly check blood pressure, a logging feature to record systolic and diastolic values, a trend analysis tool to visualize blood pressure trends over time, and articles focused on managing health as one ages.

This project was born out of a personal need to help my parents manage their health more effectively. The BP Analyzer simplifies the process of checking blood pressure and interpreting the results, especially for those who might struggle with the technical aspects. Using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a bit of backend integration, I created a user-friendly platform that empowers users to take control of their health.
